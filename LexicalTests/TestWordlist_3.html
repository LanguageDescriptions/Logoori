Logoori wordlist (test version 3)

This is a test list of words in Logoori, where the point of the test is to check its utility for a non-linguist audience of Logoori speakers. In all versions, there is a recording of the word being pronounced (the button "play" in the first column), then a transcription of the utterance, a part of speech abbreviation (n="noun", v="Verb"), the standard noun class number in the case of a noun, the stem in a simplified version (removing diacritics and other features that tend to be omitted in writing) then finally an English translation / explanation.

You can use your browser search function to locate a word, but there are only a handful of words in the current list. The words in this version are sorted alphabetically according to the stem.
