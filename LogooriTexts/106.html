Ndatanga komoloma kuli mang'ana gali ho kale ni limenya lia vandu vamenya kale. Ndanga nu kumanya lwa kwatanga mu limenya anoho mu livola kwidinyilitsa munyumba ya Nyasaye gwali muhiga wa 1948 na konyola vandu vagimila lisuvila ligali mu livugana lia nyasaye. Na livugana line yilio vandu vatanga lisala vivungelitsa vatsia munyumba yu mukere wa vamanya vatsi kusala mu. Vatsitsanga kusala nu kwitakasa, nu kwenyelitsa, kovola kulindi makuva ga pentecost. Lwa vamenya mu lisala mu nyumba vamala madiku kutula jumatatu paga jumapili ma vava vamiliyi lilinda liavo. Na venyelitsanga kusala, kolola roho mtakatifu. Na vandu valala vali vakikili kumanya roho mtakatifu na vandi navo vava vanyola roho mtakatifu. Na vigitsanga vandi kuli vanyala konyola roho mtakatifu. Vavolanga wiganili vwoni vuvwo kutula lwa wali nukili mwana mudi. Wakola viu lwana na lwuduchi muhindila wakakola mang'ana gu luhindila gataveye malahi na gaveye malahi. Wiganila kutula lwa wali mwana vindu via wakola nuveye mwanawiganila. Wiganila nulila na nusala na wasigama lwa wasigama nukuba tsikwili notevana ku nanyole ndi roho tsanangwa. na wasala nulila nu wenya konyola roho mtakatifu novelela nulila maliga na olokole lutura. Wamanya nu wenyelitsa ma uveye mu lusala walola luheni ni lutsa ni lwaka hagati hoho. Nu wallora luheni ma uhula ligali mwoyo gugwo gutsitsa ligali ma olola wakanyola roho mutakatifu. Na wakanyola roho mtakatigu vakovola wilindi, utasemana dave, utanyola mundu navichi kindu chiche uvuguli dave. ma vakohetsa malago gene yago ma malago gene yago ogenda mu na nugimila malago gene yago nogenda mu na nusala nyasaye. kali mundu nakosema ololatsa nu vukinu; wenyanga komolonda dave. Na vandu vamenya kuduka mu 1948-1950 vandu vavugula lisuvila lia yahova nyasaye. Vahula ni vamenyanga nu vuyanzi vahula mundu siyima undi kindu dave. Kwa kimba tsinyimbu tsiene yitsi vamanya vatsia koleta vitabu vyi tsinyimbu. vavola vandu vasoma mwimbange ku tsinyimbu tsie kitabu ni mwitsominya yahova nyasaye ni mumenya mu mang'ana ga nyasaye. Yilwo lwa vandu vamanyi vagimila mang'ana ga nyasaye, vatsia kwilwatsa mu Nyang'ori, vamenya mu vavugula ivangeli ku lelo vandu vatsie valangwe vilwatsi. Yilwo lwa vilwatsi vamanyi vava vanyinge vavola mu lakalole ling'ana lia nyasaye. Inyinga ya mulilola vandu vamali kuvugulamang'ana manyinge ge lilova ni vandu valomba tsidini tsiavo ni mumanya inyinga ye keheli iveye ahimbe. Ma nivavola mundu wa vugula likuva lia nyasaye wumbaka inyumba yeye ku lwanda afwanananga mundu wa kristo wa nyasaye walanga munyumba ya nyasaye na sialikola mu madamanu dave. Agendanga nasomwa imbili ni imgongo. ma nivadinyilitsa vavola musome ivangeli ma vuli mundu yavugulanga ivengeli nohomba luvaso lwilwe lumukonya mu limenya. Na nivakwigitsa mu Zaburi moja ivolanga: aveye nu vugasu mundu uvula kogenda mu makuva ga voni. Kandi sia yikala mu kisago cha va kehenyehenye navutsa ayanza mima jia yahova naganagana mima jijye mbasu nu vudiku alfwanana nu musala gwa simikwa himbe hi migera jia amatsi gwamanga ku muhiga gwa gwo, matu ga gwo siganiala mba, viosi via akola vilagasa. Nivakudinyilitsa mu luvasu yilwo, ma kandi kosomanga mu kitabu cha Yohana 14 kekovola mutahenda mioyo jyinyu, musuvili nyasaye, musuvili inze kandi munyumba ya dada wange eve no vomenyo vunyinge nitaveye ndio ngani ndamuvolela nzitsa kumulombela vogono. Nzie kandi ndatsa kumuvugula. Mu luvasu yilwo mundu yasomanga ku yahula ndio yahula naveye nu vuyanzi vonene, kandi yahula niyitsominya nyasaye, ma kandi yagimila malago, ni vavola ni ugimiyi malago likomi: utaheya, utiiva, utakaywa kindu cha mutende wovo, utakaywa kali mukana mutugwa wu mutende wovo; ma mang'ana yago ga kwitsuraanga hagati hi limenya lyitu. Ma kwamenya no komenyanga mu vukristayo vwa nyasaye. Kwa vandu vali va kale vali ni miyinzi jiadukana. Nivagimila lilago valeka ku dave. Vakovola noleka lilago yili si ulava mukristayo mulahi mba. Mukristayo yumbaka inyumba yeye ku luanda; ahulitsa mang'ana ga vandu vavola dave, ahulitsanga tsimbemba dave, agendanga na vandu vadamanu dave, yikala mu muvidieli dave. Yikala hango hehe naganagana kuyinzila miyinzi jijie namanya eng'ombe yaynge yahi, avana vange valalia, ndalima mulimi, ndataga mu tsingutsa? Ku kwamenya ni kwava vakristayo va nyasaye yicho kijira ni kogenda mu inzila ya nyasaye. Suvilanga mang'ana gange gaheri ndio mu lyidinyilitsa liu vukristayo vwa nyasaye. Sande.

Life in the past

I will start by talking about the things in the past, and how people were living in the past. I will start by recognizing, the way we began in life and strengthening the house of God in the year 1948. They took great faith in the house of God. It was started by white men, they went to a woman's house and they owner knew that they had come to pray. They used to go and pray as they cleansed themselves. And they had an aim of keeping the Pentecostal things. While they were staying in the house they spent in there from Monday to Sunday and they had led in their leadership. They wanted to pray and see the Holy Spirit. Some people had not known the Holy Spirit and some had received the Holy Spirit. And they taught others how they could receive Holy Spirit. They asked them to repent their sins from their childhood. You used to do childish things and since you are grown now, you have done some things that are good while the others are not good. You repent sins that you did since your childhood. You repent as you cry and you refuse, you also kneel down. You knelt down as you cry and ask how you will receive Holy Spirit. And you were praying as you cry in need of Holy Spirit, you shed tears. If you wanted to know that you've received the Holy Spirit, you used to see the lightning shining where you were. You used to see the lightning, and your heart goes deep, then you get Holy Spirit. When you receive the Holy Spirit, you were to take care of yourself; you should not speak bad about the others. When you find someone has left his/her property do not steal it. They used to give us those instructions and you walk as you keep them. You pray as you walk, even if someone speaks against you, you just take is as a joke. You do not want to follow him. People from the year 1948-1950 had great faith in God. They felt that they were living with joy, one could not deny the other one something. It was joy, they organized for their church then they sing their songs as they pray, this was one of the songs:

Today I am standing with the angels

I am standing in front and I have accepted

They used to sing like that and they knew. When they got to the year 1952 a white man came from abroad and stayed in this land. His name was Clark and he came and found that people didn't have something to eat. When he came to the church, he used to preach that we Christians should wash our cloths and look as the people of God. When you appear as the people of God, the word of God will stay in you, and you wont be cheated or criticized. He used to teach as everyone sees. After teaching then he says to his servants "go and look for a place where you can cook tea" then they start to prepare tea. Then he asks all the people to sit down and take tea. Some people didn't know how to drink tea. Long time when the sugar was there they were using lemon leaves. After putting the leaves on the cooking pan, he then adds in water, they were not adding in milk.

But when the white men came, they asked them to be taking tea that has tealeaves. That is when people knew about adding milk to the tea. And we started knowing if the sugar was enough or not. They used to ensure that people have taken tea well. People now knew tea, until they discovered that Christianity is good. People used to try their level best, saying that they want to go to the church and find the white man with the tea prepared and they will take it. People used to rush and they fill the church, they were prayed for and the people received the Holy Spirit. And you find people singing gospel songs. They were singing as they praised God. After singing these songs they used to go and bring hymnbooks. Then they say that the people who are able to read they sing those songs from the books as they praise God and live in the things of the lord. That is when people learned the word of God and they went to preach in Nyang'ori they were staying there than they take the bible then they go and call people and they preach. That is how the preachers became many and they asked people to see the word of God. The time when you will see people have taken the things of the world and making their own religions. That is when you will know the destruction is near. Then they said that someone who took the word of god and builds it in a rock is like the Christian that God has called and he will never do anything bad. He is walking as he is read from the front to the back. They encouraged everyone to be reading the bible. Everyone took the bible and began reading his verse that assisted him in his life. And they taught us that psalm 1 says: he has blessings, he who does not walk in the counsel of wickedness. He does not seat of mockers, but loves the ways of the lord and meditates upon them day and night, he is like a tree planted by the stream of water that never lacks water nor withers, everything that he does he prospers. They encourage us with the verse. We again read from the book of John 14 that says: you should not be afraid in your hearts, you believe in God, believe in me, in my father's house there are many mansion rooms. If it were not for that I am telling you that I am going to make a place for you. I will go and return back to take you. Whenever someone read that verse he felt that he had a lot of joy. He also felt that he was praising God. He also took heed of the laws. They used to say that you keep the 10 commandments. You should not commit adultery, you should not steal, you should not covet your neighbor's property, and you should not covet a girl. Those are the things that filled our lives. We lived in the salvation of Christ. People in the past had enough activities. Whenever they hold the law, they never left it. They were telling us that if you loose the law then you are not a good Christian. A Christians builds his house on a rock, he does not listen to what the people say, he does not listen to bad things that people speak against the others. He does not walk with bad people. He does not sit in bad companies. He stays at home thinking about his tasks, if his cow has been fed, if his children have eaten, will he be able to do farming, will he plant vegetables? We used to live like that, we are believers of Christ and that is the reason to why we are walking in the ways of Christ. I believe my words have ended there in emphasizing the salvation of Christ.

Thank you.
