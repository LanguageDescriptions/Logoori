Masayi Ga Yesu

Esther Mmbone

Shi ga li masahi

Getsinyama tsyosi,

Ga nyala kwogidza vwoni

Nokuvuturidza

Masahi ga yesu

Ga nyala kwogidza

Vwoni vwange vwosi bei

Nokulavidza myoyo

Yesu ni shihaanwa

Shilahi munono

Shya sira ligali muno,

vihaanwa vyavandi

Mukonyi mulahi

Ndolanga musigo,

Gwa u gingi ku musala,

Gwa li vwoni vwange

Nda suuvira iwe

Lwa mmali kweyama

Na nzeganira vwoni

Nda samefwa vwangu

The blood of Jesus

It is not all blood

From all animals

That can wash away my sin

And remove it

It is the blood of Jesus

That can wash away

All my sin

And clean souls

Jesus is a great gift

Great gift

That skipped at length

All other gifts

A great helper

I see a baggage,

That you carried on the tree

It was my sin

I believed in you

After finishing to accept

I repented my sin

I was forgiven very fast
