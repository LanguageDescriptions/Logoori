Siasa Mu Kenya 3

Herman Asava

Manga'na ga isiasa mu Kenya muno, Tom Mboya iye wu kwashauriana nangaye natsia Amereka natsia nagonya Amereka, Amereka vatsa nivatsia nende vangeresa. Ma vangeresa vavugilila vakuha vwami muno. Vamereka ni vandu vali ni kivune sana yivo vali vanyala kokonya kivala kinu. Lwa ndola vandu vange ni viluka ku Amereka, si mbula vulahi dave. AMereka yatuga tsigavila tsinyinge kandi yiyo yakokonya kuluha konyola makanisa gaveye muno manyinge yaga, konyola tsisukulu tsinyinge muno; kali lwa olola avana vitu vatula muno vatsia Amereka, lwa aduchi tsa Amereka yilana yinu dave. Amanya avola kivala cha Kenya ku nakengonye kindiki. Lakini Amereka eveye no vokonyi vunyinge vujila vukabila dave. Tsihiri tsiosi tsie lilova tsifwera Amereka yila. Ku inze ndakayanzi vamereka vaveye nu mwoyo gwene gwali gula; kali vananga Amereka ndatsia yo ndakayanzi sana. Kali vambugula muno ningiyinzila muno venyanga nzie komenya Amereka lakini si ndamanya ninzia nangavo dave. Vamereka venyanga nzie nangavo engereka ila. Miyinzi jia ndayinzila muno minyinge Vamereka yivo vambetsa miyinzi jiene jila. Ku sanditsa eserekali ya Amereka itava ku nu vudunyi ni kunyi dave; niganyalika kali vanyala kunanga nzie kuyo male kuyo mweli mulala anoho madiku ganga nizitsominye eserekali ya Amereka. Na mwana mwene Obama uveye kondeve uyu, vandu vitu vamuhe pongezi kijira mwana mwene anyala kokonya kivala vulahi sana. Lwa kwateva Amereka vatsa vakokonya nonyola munu vuhuru kwamanya kuvugula yo Musungu mulala walange lawyer; vamulanga Sir Marshall iye yali lawyer witu, iye yakokonya ku Lancaster House nakugilung'anyia mang'ana gene yago. Na ikatiba ya kwali nangayo yene yila, Vamereka yivo vakokonya kuva ni ikatiba yayamihanji Kenya yeyo. Sande.
