Muhonja Wetu

Alice Mugasia

Madiku ga kale kwali ho nu muyayi undi vamulanga Musa na mukana wavo vamulanga Muhonja. Musa yali yayanza kwaitsa tsing'ombe na vivuli vavo vali vakutsa vatigara tsa vavili ndio. Ku Musa yatsitsa kwaitsa ni yilani sasa Muhonja alamwigulila muliango. Ku yatsa ku muliango ma asingila ku ma atanga kwimba:

Muhonja witu Muhonja*2

Ndula kwaya, na

Niviayo viosi, na

Ni vya magondi, na

Na ndula kutula, na

Muhonja witu mulala

Unzigulili na

Ma Muhonja yigula muliango alia ma kandi yilana. Yakilana Muhonja ahana muliango. Linani limanya litse litange kwimba:

Muhonja witu Muhonja*2

Ndula kwaya, na

Niviayo viosi, na

Ni vya magondi, na

Na ndula kutula, na

Muhonja witu mulala

Unzigulili na

Muhonja amuvolela "aye, yive ni isauti ya Musa mba." Ma litsia. Ku lelo liamanya litsia ku muganga liteva "sasa nyala kokola ndi nzimbi isauti kuli ya Musa?" Muganga yamanya namuvolela utsie unwe ku vwuki, wakanwa ku vukwi mwoyo gumanya gwikuluki. Lwa yamanya atsie yamanya natanga lyimba Musa yakatsia. Natanga:

Muhonja witu Muhonja*2

Ndula kwaya, na

Niviayo viosi, na

Ni vya magondi, na

Ndula kutula, na

Kutula matimu na

Kwitire vasigu

Muhonja yamanya niyigula na lwayigula linani liamanya limumile. Lwa liamumila sasa Musa yamanya niyatsa na lwayayatsa natanga lyimba:

Muhonja witu Muhonja*2

Ndula kwaya, na

Niviayo viosi, na

Nahulilitsa malini mundu yigula dave. Lelo lwa yabomola muliango nanyola Muhonja linani liakumila. Yamanya natevelitsa natsia nanyola linani liveye tsa havundu navugula lubanga. Lwa yatsia aliteme, naliteva "ndeme yaha?" Nalio likola "halulu halulu." Lwa yamanya ateme munda Muhonja yamanya natula mu ma nivatula nivagaluka yengo. Lugano lwa luheli yaho.

Our Muhonja

Long time ago we had a boy by the name Musa and his sister was called Muhonja. Musa liked grazing cows and their parents had died leaving them alone. Musa continued to graze the cows and when the he came back home, Muhonja used to open the door for him. He used to come at the door then he starts to sing:

Our Muhonja*2

Am from grazing the cows, and

They are all the grazing place, and

They are for the sheep, and

And I am from there, and

Our only Muhonja

Open for me and

Then Muhonja could open the door. Then he eats and returns to the grazing place. Whenever he returned Muhonja used to close the door. An ogre used to come and begins to sing:

Our Muhonja*2

Am from grazing the cows, and

They are all the grazing place, and

They are for the sheep, and

And I am from there, and

Our only Muhonja

Open for me and

Muhonja used to tell him "no, you do not sound like Moses." The ogre could leave. The ogre went to a witch and asked him what he could do to have a voice like the one of Musa. The witch asked him to go and take honey, if he takes the honey the voice will be smooth. He used to go and starts to sing when Musa left. Then he begins:

Our Muhonja*2

Am from grazing the cows, and

They are all the grazing place, and

They are for the sheep, and

And I am from there, and

Our only Muhonja

Open for me and

Muhonja opened the door, when he opened the ogre swallowed her. After the ogre swallowed her Musa came and began to sing:

Our Muhonja*2

Am from grazing the cows, and

They are all the grazing place, and

They are for the sheep, and

And I am from there, and

Our only Muhonja

Open for me and

He listened keenly, but someone didn't open. When he broke the door, he found that the ogre had swallowed his sister Muhonja. He asked where the ogre was and he found the ogre, he took the panga when he went to cut him, he asked where to cut and the ogre said "it is painful, it is painful" when he cut the stomach Muhonja came out. And they returned home. My story has ended there.
